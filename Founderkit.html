Overview

FounderKit was a product that was developed in-house at Super, a 4 man startup studio led by two YC founders. In my 8 months there, it was the product that gained the most traction. (10k users, 60k ARR)

FounderKit was a product that sourced tool recommendations and reviews from experienced founders so that young founders can quickly discover the best tool to use tp build their startup. We quickly became viral in the entrepreneurship community due to it solving a real need for first-time founders.

I joined the team post-MVP and worked to redesign the product to handle v2 features while concurrently developing the community.

However, the product was considered unmonetizable mid-redesign and efforts to work on it was shelved in favor of other, higher potential, ventures. I left the team soon after to pursue other ventures.

The product is currently on hiatus.
