Supporting First Time Founders

FounderKit

Spring/Summmer 2017

FounderKit was a product that was developed in-house at Super, a 4 man startup studio led by two YC founders. (10k users, 60k ARR)

FounderKit was a community for young founders to discover the best tools to use to grow their company from experienced founders. We quickly became viral in the entrepreneurship community due to our high signal-noise ratio.

I started work on this product post-MVP and was working on a major redesign of the product to handle v2 features. However, the product was considered lacking upside mid-redesign and efforts to work on it was shelved in favor of other ventures.
