Hello.

I like lunches at quiet cafes and working with amazing founders.

I’ve created at VC firms (Dorm Room Fund), early-stage startups (Fetchum, Pison), startup studios (Super), and for billion-dollar companies (AppDynamics, LimeBike, Royal Caribbean).

I believe in abstracting ideas to their base principles and listening to others' stories as frameworks for designing great products.

Right now, I’m exploring how can we enable mass-consumer adoption of AR by designing a future with a love for humanity at its core. Sometimes, I record my thoughts on it.
