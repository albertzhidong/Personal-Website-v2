Hello! I'm Albert.

I like lunches at quiet cafes and working with amazing founders.

I’ve been fortunate to have the opportunity to design at VC funds (Dorm Room Fund), early-stage startups (Pison, Fetchum, Miracle), startup studios (Super), and for billion-dollar companies (AppDynamics, LimeBike, Royal Caribbean).

Right now, I’m exploring how can we enable mass-consumer adoption of AR by designing its future with a love for humanity at its core. Sometimes, I record my thoughts on it.
