Overview

As a freshmen, a group of us aimed to create an Asian-American identity in Greek life at Brown. Partially for what you would expect a group of freshmen boys to want out of a fraternity and partially to use it as a vehicle to shed light on Asian-American issues in the community.

Portrayal of identity is unique to each individual and each collective though. If we were to use FDS as a vehicle to shed light on these issues, then that means these issues would be need to be presented within the framework of the FDS ethos and the greater Greek life ethos. Yet, without the institutional support, how loud can our individual voices be? And more importantly, how much tangible change can we enact?

On hiatus as of Spring 2017.
