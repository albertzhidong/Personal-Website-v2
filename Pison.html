Designing the Future of Gesture

Pison

Fall/Winter 2019

Pison is building electroneurographic based hardware that allows for the control of devices through nerve and muscle signals. I joined as the first gestural interaction designer (part-time) and worked to prototype gestural interfaces for use cases with the US Army, NASA, and AR glasses.

Concrete results are hidden behind an NDA. Happy to chat about high-level abstractions offline anytime.
